About the client
Wind Rises Dubai is a sailing club dedicated to promoting cost-effective and environmentally-friendly sailing experiences. With a focus on sustainability, Wind Rises Dubai offers a range of sailing activities and programs designed to minimize environmental impact while increasing the thrill of being out on the water and enjoying the beauty of Dubai’s coastline.

About the client

Wind Rises Dubai is a sailing club dedicated to promoting cost-effective and environmentally-friendly sailing experiences. With a focus on sustainability, Wind Rises Dubai offers a range of sailing activities and programs designed to minimize environmental impact while increasing the thrill of being out on the water and enjoying the beauty of Dubai’s coastline.

Goal

To increase recognition of the brand and showcase its unique services.

What we did

Pitched a press release

The journey started with the announcement that Wind Rises secured $ 1M in pre-seed funding.

Pitching the press release to business media was essential to highlight the company’s intention to grow in the region. The news appeared in Arabian Business, Trading View, NABD, Zawya, Mubasher, Enterprise News, Fortune Arabia, etc.

Got journalists and influencers involved

The next step was to invite leading journalists to show them firsthand Wind Rises' Sailing Experience, with no expectations from our side.

We introduced the brand to influencers and invited them to ride a sailboat.
