About the client

Wize, founded in 2022, is a B2B platform for delivery and retail business. Wize ecosystem includes several services: electric motorcycles, rental and subscription platform for business owners, battery-as-a-service and swapping stations and software components.

Goal

To draw attention to the startup and introduce it to potential clients, partners and product investors.

What we did

Created the press release

We started with an excellent news about pre-seed funding round co-led by MENA-focused angel investors. Our press release presented a diverse review of the startup’s solutions and its plans for the region. We addressed particular issues the business is facing, mentioned technological effectiveness and emphasized sustainability.

Forbes Middle East was the first media to publish the news. Then we got more than 10 reprints in such outlets as Wamda, Inc.Arabia and Zawya.

One of the first publications about the startup

Pitched interviews, comments and features

After the first publication, we began pitching other media to share the story behind the startup. Received responses from Arabian Gulf Business Insight, which agreed to interview Wize co-founder. Wize also got featured in top business media of Saudi Arabia, Arab News. On top of that, Your Story and Arabian Business published articles about the startup.

In addition, we assisted Wize run its LinkedIn account by drafting posts and updating medial plan weekly.

Results

During 3 months of cooperation with Wize, we had over 15 publications in local media: news, interviews, comments. The team got tons of incoming requests and congratulations from partners, potential employees and collegues. Today the team is collaborating with leading delivery services in the MENA region!

Fast Company ME also asked the brand to comment on UAE’s last-mile delivery. Several months later journalists still ask the company to share their view of sustainable delivery in the region.
