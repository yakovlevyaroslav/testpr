About the client

Yalla Market is a quick-commerce startup that provides grocery delivery services. Based in the UAE, Yalla Market aims to deliver groceries and other essentials to customers quickly, promising delivery within 15 minutes. Yalla Market is a trendsetter in the quick-commerce sector, focusing on convenience and speed to meet the needs of busy urban consumers. YallaHub became a new course in the company's developments helping brands to enter the international MENA market.

As a result of the company’s launch news, we were featured in the TIER-1 media: Khaleej Times, Zawya, GCC Business News, and many more!
